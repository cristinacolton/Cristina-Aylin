Discovering Through Sound


    Cristina Aylin blends hip hop beats, lush synths, jazzy chords, and smooth vocals. As an independent artist, Cristina is always searching for something new, whether it's a fresh synth pattern, an innovative chord progression, or a unique genre fusion. Cristina's music captures her adventurous spirit. When she's not in the studio, she's exploring the mountains or picking up new hobbies, bringing that sense of discovery back into her music. Her songs offer a unique mix of styles, delivering depth and texture that stands out in the indie music scene.

        Cristina is a captivating performer who brings a sense of calmness, soulful energy & creativity to every show. Her versatility makes her a perfect fit for gigs, collaborations, playlists, and music blogs. If you're looking for an artist who embodies curiosity and originality, Cristina Aylin is the one to watch.
